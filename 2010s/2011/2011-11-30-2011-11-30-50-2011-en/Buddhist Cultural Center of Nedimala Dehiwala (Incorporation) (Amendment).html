AN ACT TO AMEND THE BUDDHIST CULTURAL CENTRE OF NEDIMALA, DEHIWALA (INCORPORATION) ACT, NO. 30 OF 2008.

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—

Short title.

1. This Act may be cited as the Buddhist Cultural Centre of Nedimala, Dehiwala (Incorporation)(Amendment) Act, No. 50 of 2011.

Amendment of section 6 of the Buddhist Cultural Centre of Nedimala, Dehiwala (Incorporation) Act, No. 30 of 2008.

2. Section 6 of the Buddhist Cultural Centre of Nedimala, Dehiwala (Incorporation) Act, No. 30 of 2008 is hereby amended by the repeal of paragraph (b) thereof and the substitution therefor of the following :—

“(b)

to borrow or raise money from government approved banks and other institutions for the purpose of the Corporation in such manner and upon such securities as the Corporation may deem fit;”.

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

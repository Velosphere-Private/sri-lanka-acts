AN ACT TO AMEND THE CEYLONESE BROTHERS OFST. JOSEPH ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 301)

WHEREAS the Society of the Ceylonese Brothers ofSt. Joseph was incorporated by the Ceylonese Brothers ofSt. Joseph Ordinance, No. 59 of 1942 :

AND WHEREAS the Society of the Ceylonese Brothers ofSt. Joseph now wish to change its name to “Society of theSri Lankan Brothers of Charity” :

AND WHEREAS the said Society of the Ceylonese Brothersof St. Joseph has now applied to have the said Ordinanceamended to give effect to such change in its name and it willbe expedient to grant such application :

BE it therefore enacted by the Parliament of the DemocraticSocialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—

1.This Act may be cited as the Society of the CeyloneseBrothers of St. Joseph (Amendment) Act, No. 4 of 2013.

2.In every context in which the Society of the CeyloneseBrothers of St. Joseph is mentioned (whether by that name orby the abbreviation of the Ceylonese Brothers of St. Josephin the Ceylonese Brothers of St. Joseph Ordinance (Chapter301), other than in the long title, or in any rule, notice orother document made or issued under the Ceylonese Brothersof St. Joseph Ordinance (Chapter 301), there shall, unless thecontext otherwise requires, be substituted for the expression“Society of the Ceylonese Brothers of St. Joseph” and for theexpression “Ceylonese Brothers of St. Joseph” the words“Society of the Sri Lankan Brothers of Charity” and the words“Sri Lankan Brothers of Charity”.

Short title.

Preamble.

“Society of theCeyloneseBrothers of St.Joseph” and“CeyloneseBrothers of St.Jeseph” to beknown as“Society of theSri LankanBrothers ofCharity” and “SriLankan Brothersof Charity”respectively.

3.(1) All contracts, agreements, certificates or otherinstruments or documents whatsoever made, issued orexecuted prior to the date of the commencement of this Act,by or in favour of the “Society of the Ceylonese Brothers ofSt. Joseph” shall be deemed on or after date of commencementof this Act, to be, and to have been made, issued or executedby or in favour of the Society of the Sri Lankan Brothers ofCharity.

(2) All suits, actions, appeals and other legal proceedingsinstituted by or against the “Society of the Ceyloness Brothersof St. Joseph” and pending on the day immediately prior tothe date of the commencement of this Act, shall not abate orbe discontinued, and may be continued or enforced by oragainst the “Society of the Sri Lankan Brothers of Charity”.

(3) All the rights, liabilities and obligations of the “Societyof the Ceylonese Brothers of St. Joseph” other than the rights,liabilities and obligations referred to in subsection (1) on theday immediately prior to the date of the commencement ofthis Act, shall be deemed to be the rights, liabilities andobligations of the “Society of the Sri Lankan Brothers ofCharity”.

4.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

Sinhala text toprevail in case ofinconsistency.

AN ACT TO AMEND THE MUNICIPAL COUNCILS ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 252)

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—

Short title and date of operation.

1. This Act may be cited as the Municipal Councils (Amendment) Act, No. 34 of 2014 and shall come into operation on such date as the Minister may appoint by Order Published in the Gazette.

Insertion of new section 215B in the

Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252).

2. The following new section is hereby inserted immediately after section 215A of the Municipal Councils Ordinance (Chapter 252) and shall have effect as section Municipal 215B of that Ordinance :—

“Transitional provision.

215 . The provisions of the proviso to section 215 of the Municipal Councils Ordinance as amended by the Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012 shall not be applicable to any budget or supplementary budget to be submitted to a Municipal Council constituted in terms of the results of elections held prior to the date of coming into operation of Local Authorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012 and Local Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act, No. 22 of 2012 respectively.”.

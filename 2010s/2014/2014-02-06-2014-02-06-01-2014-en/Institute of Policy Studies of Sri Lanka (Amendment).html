AN ACT TO AMEND THE INSTITUTE OF POLICY STUDIES OF SRI LANKA

ACT, NO. 53 OF 1988

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—

Short title.

1. This Act may be cited as the Institute of Policy Studies of Sri Lanka (Amendment) Act, No. 1 of 2014.

Amendment of Section 7 of Act, No.53 of 1988.

2. Section 7 of the Institute of Policy Studies of Sri Lanka Act, No. 53 of 1988 is hereby amended by the repeal of paragraph (c) of that section, and the substitution therefor of the following paragraph:—

“(c)

the Governor of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka or an officer nominated by the Governor;”.

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

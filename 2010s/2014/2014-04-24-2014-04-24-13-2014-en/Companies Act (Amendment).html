AN ACT TO AMEND THE COMPANIES

ACT, NO. 7 OF 2007

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :-

Short title.

1. This Act may be cited as the Companies (Amendment) Act, No. 13 of 2014 as follows :—

Amendment of section 132 of the Companies Act, No.7 of 2007.

2. Section 132 of the Companies Act, No. 7 of 2007 (hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) is hereby amended by the repeal of that section and the substitution therefore of the following new section :—

Amendment of Ninth Schedule to the principal enactment.

3. The item 2 of the Ninth Schedule to the principal enactment is hereby amended as follows :–

(1) in paragraph (b), by the substitution for the words “chargeable for one complete year prior to the commencement of the liquidation, that year to be selected” of the words “chargeable for five complete years prior to the commencement of the liquidation, that five year period to be selected”;

(2) in paragraph (d), by the repeal of that paragraph and substitution therefor of the following new paragraph :—

“(d) value added tax charged or chargeable for taxable periods within five year period prior to the commencement of the liquidation, such taxable periods to be selected by the Commissioner-General of Inland Revenue in accordance with the provisions of the Value Added Tax Act, No. 14 of 2002;”.

Amendment of section 529 of the principal enactment.

4. Section 529 of the principal enactment is hereby amended by the insertion immediately after the definition of the expression “class” of the following definition :—

“Commissioner-General of Inland Revenue” means the Commissioner-General of Inland Revenue appointed under section 208 of the Inland Revenue Act, No. 10 of 2006.

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency.

5. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text Shall prevail.

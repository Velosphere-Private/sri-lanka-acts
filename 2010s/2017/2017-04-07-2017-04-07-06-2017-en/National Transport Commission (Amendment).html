AN ACT TO AMEND THE NATIONAL TRANSPORT COMMISSION

ACT, NO. 37 OF 1991

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—

Short title.

1. This Act may be cited as the National Transport Commission (Amendment) Act, No. 6 of 2017.

Amendment of section 40 of Act, No. 37 of 1991.

2. Section 40 of the National Transport Commission Act, No. 37 of 1991 is hereby amended by the substitution for the words “not exceeding ten thousand rupees” of the words “not exceeding two hundred thousand rupees”.

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

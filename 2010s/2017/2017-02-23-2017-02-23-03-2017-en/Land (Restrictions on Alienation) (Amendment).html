AN ACT TO AMEND THE LAND (RESTRICTIONS ON ALIENATION)

ACT, NO. 38 OF 2014

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—

1. This Act may be cited as the Land (Restrictions on Alienation) (Amendment) Act, No. 3 of 2017 and shall be

Short title and date of deemed to have come into operation with effect from operation. January 8, 2017.

2. The following new section is hereby inserted immediately after section 5 of the Land (Restrictions on

Insertion of new section 5A in Act, Alienation) Act, No. 38 of 2014 and shall have effect as No. 38 of 2014. section 5A of that Act:—

5A. Notwithstanding anything to the contrary

“Land Lease Tax not to be in any of the provisions of this Act, the levied with effect from January 8, 2017. (a)

provisions relating to the Land Lease Tax shall not apply to a lease of any land—

to a foreigner; or

(b) to a company incorporated in Sri Lanka under the Companies Act, where any foreign shareholding in such company, either direct or indirect, is fifty per cent or above; or

(c) to a foreign company,

under and indenture of lease executed on or after January 8, 2017, and accordingly the Land Lease Tax shall not be charged, levied or collected from any such person or company on or after such date.”.

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

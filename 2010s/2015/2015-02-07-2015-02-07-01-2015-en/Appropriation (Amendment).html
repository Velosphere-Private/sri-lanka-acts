AN ACT TO AMEND THE APPROPRIATION ACT, NO. 41 OF 2014

Short title.

Amendment of section 2 of Act, No. 41 of 2014.

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :— 1. This Act may be cited as the Appropriation (Amendment) Act, No. 1 of 2015. 2.

AN ACT TO AMEND THE IMMIGRANTS AND EMIGRANTS ACT (CHAPTER 351)

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—

Short title.

1. This Act may be cited as the Immigrants and Emigrants (Amendment) Act, No. 7 of 2015.

Insertion of new section 35A in Chapter 351.

2. The Immigrants and Emigrants Act (Chapter 351) is hereby amended by the insertion, immediately after section 35 thereof, of the following new section:—

“Taking of finger printing for passport purposes.

35A. The Authority prescribed to issue or renew passports may take finger printing for the inclusion in the passport, from a person applying for a passport or the renewal of a passport.”.

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

AN ACT TO AMEND THE VALUE ADDED TAX ACT, NO. 14 OF 2002

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-

Short title and the date of operation.

1. (1) This Act may be cited as the Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of 2015 and subject to subsection (2) of this section, shall be deemed to have come into operation on January 1, 2015 unless the date on which certain provisions shall come into operation are specified in such sections.

(2) The amendments made to section 17, 19, 21, 25A(5), 26, 28, 34, 41, 44, 47 and 66 by this Act shall come into operation on such date as the Minister may appoint by Order published in the Gazette.

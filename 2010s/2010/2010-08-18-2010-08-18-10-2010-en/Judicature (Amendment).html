AN ACT TO AMEND THE JUDICATURE ACT, NO. 2 OF 1978

Be it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—

Short title and date of operation.

1. This Act may be cited as the Judicature (Amendment) Act, No. 10 of 2010 and shall come into operation on such date as the Minister may, by Order published in the Gazette appoint.

Amendment of section 4 of Act No. 2 of 1978.

2. Section 4 of the Judicature Act, No. 2 of 1978 is hereby amended in paragraph (a) of that section, by the substitution for the words “not more than sixty Judges,” of the words “not more than seventy-five Judges,”.

Sinhala texts to prevail in case of inconsistency.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

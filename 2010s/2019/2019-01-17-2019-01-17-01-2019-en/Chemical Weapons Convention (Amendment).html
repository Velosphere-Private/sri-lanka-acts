L.D.—O. 23/2018

AN ACT TO AMEND THE CHEMICAL WEAPONS CONVENTION

ACT, NO. 58 OF 2007

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—

Short title.

1. This Act may be cited as the Chemical Weapons Convention (Amendment) Act, No. 1 of 2019.

2. Section 6 of the Chemical Weapons Convention Act, Amendment of section 6 of Act, No. 58 of 2007.

No. 58 of 2007 is hereby amended in subsection (1) thereof by the substitution for the words “Minister in charge of the subject of Industries” of the words “Minister assigned the subject of Defence”.

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

L.D.—O. 37/2014

AN ACT TO AMEND THE INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

ACT, NO. 36 OF 2003

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—

Short title.

1. This Act may be cited as the Intellectual Property (Amendment) Act, No. 7 of 2018.

Amendment of section 161 of the Intellectual Property Act, No. 36 of 2003.

2. Section 161 of the Intellectual Property Act, No. 36 of 2003 is hereby amended by the insertion immediately after subsection (4) thereof, the following new subsection:—

“(4A) The Minister may prescribe any geographical indication in respect of any goods or products for the purpose of this Act.”.

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

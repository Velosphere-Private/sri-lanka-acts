AN ACT TO INCORPORATE THE PERIYA MOULANA, ZAINMOULANA SHRINES AND THAKIYAS DEVELOPMENT SOCIETY

WHEREAS a Development Society called and Known asthe “Periya Moulana, Zain Moulana Shrines and ThakiyasDevelopment Society heretofore been formed for thepurpose of effectually carrying out and transacting all objectsand matters connected with the said Development Societyaccording to the rules agreed to by its members:

AND WHEREAS the said Development Society hasheretofore successfully carried out and transacted theseveral objects for which it was formed and has applied to beincorporated and it will be for the public advantage to grantthe application:

BE it therefore enacted by the Parliament of the DemocraticSocialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-

1.This Act may be cited as the Periya Moulana, ZainMoulana Shrines and Thakiyas Development Society(Incorporation) Act, No.26 of 1999.

2.From and after the date of commencement of thisAct, such and so many persons as now are the members ofthe Periya Moulana, Zain Moulana Shrines and ThakiyasDevelopment Society (hereinafter referred to as the“Development Society”) or shall hereafter be admittedmembers of the Corporation hereby constituted, shall be abody corporate (hereinafter referred to as the “Corporation”)with perpetual succession, under the name and style of the“Periya Moulana, Zain Moulana Shrines and ThakiyasDevelopment Society” and by that name may sue and be suedin all courts, with full power and authority to have and use acommon seal and alter the same at its pleasure.

3.The general objects for which the Corporation isconstituted are hereby declared to be -(a) to assist in the rehabilitation, maintenance,development and upkeep of the Shrines andThakiyas and the places of Muslim religiouspilgrimage and resort ;

(b) to promote the welfare, comfort and well being ofpilgrims who may visit, venerate or seek succourand rest in, such places of pilgrimage, or who de-sire to observe Islamic rules of worship and prayer;

(c) to promote in general, the religious, cultural,educational and economic well being and Welfareof Muslims ;

(d) to provide relief and aid to the poor, the needy thesick and the aged of the Muslim faith visiting theShrines and Thakiyas, and any other relief such asthe provisions of social services, Educational andTraining facilities of all manner and description tothe Muslim Community in general ;

(e) to establish and maintain a Muslim religious schoolfor the teaching of Quran and the Arabic Language;and

(f) do such other acts and things as are conducive orincidental to the attainment of all or any of the aboveobjects.

4.Subject to the provisions of this Act, and any otherwritten law, the Corporation shall have the power to do,perform and execute all such acts, matters and thingswhatsoever, as are necessary or desirable for the promotionand furtherance of the objects of the Corporation or any oneof them , including the power to open, operate or close bankaccounts, to borrow or raise moneys, with or without security,to receive or collect grants and donations, to invest andexpend its funds, and to engage employ and dismiss officersand servants required for the carrying out of the objects ofCorporation.

5. (1) The affairs of the Corporation shall subject to therules in force for the time being of the Corporation, beadministered by a Board of Trustees elected in accordancewith such rules.

(2) The first Board of Trustees of the Corporation shallbe the Board of Trustees of the Development Society holdingoffice on the day preceding the date of commencement of thisAct and whose names are specified in the Schedule to thisAct.

6.(1) It shall be lawful for the Corporation, from time totime, at any general meeting and by a majority of the members who are the descendants of the Seyed Zain SakhaffMoulana family present and voting, to make rules not incon-sistent with the provisions of this act and any other writtenlaw, for the admission , withdrawal and expulsion of membersof the Corporation, for the election of the office-bearers andthe Board of Trustees and their powers conduct and duties,for the procedure to be followed at the meetings of theCorporation and the Board of Trustees and generally, for themanagement of the affairs of the Corporation and theaccomplishment of its objects. Such rules when made may, ata like meeting and in like manner be altered, added to, amendedor rescinded.

(2) The members of the Corporation shall be subject tothe rules of the Corporation.

7.The Corporation shall be able and capable in law, toacquire and hold any property, movable or immovable, whichmay become vested in it by virture of any purchase, grant,gift, testamentary disposition or otherwise and all suchproperty shall be held by the Corporation for the purposes ofthis Act and subject to the rules in force for the time being ofthe Corporation, with full power to sell, mortgage, lease,exchange or otherwise dispose of the same.

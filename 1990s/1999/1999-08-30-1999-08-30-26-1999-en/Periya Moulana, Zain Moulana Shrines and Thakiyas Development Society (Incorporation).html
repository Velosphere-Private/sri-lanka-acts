Periya Moulana, Zain Moulana Shrines and Thakiyas Development Society (Incorporation) Act, No. 26 of 1999 [Certified on 30 th August , 1999] L.D.- O. 58/96.

AN ACT TO INCORPORATE THE PERIYA MOULANA, ZAIN MOULANA SHRINES AND THAKIYAS DEVELOPMENT SOCIETY

Preamble

WHEREAS a Development Society called and Known as the “Periya Moulana, Zain Moulana Shrines and Thakiyas Development Society heretofore been formed for the purpose of effectually carrying out and transacting all objects and matters connected with the said Development Society according to the rules agreed to by its members:

AND WHEREAS the said Development Society has heretofore successfully carried out and transacted the several objects for which it was formed and has applied to be incorporated and it will be for the public advantage to grant the application:

BE it therefore enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-

General objects of the Corporation.

  1. constituted are hereby declared to be -
  2. to assist in the rehabilitation, maintenance, development and upkeep of the Shrines and Thakiyas and the places of Muslim religious pilgrimage and resort ;

The general objects for which the Corporation is

(b) to promote the welfare, comfort and well being of pilgrims who may visit, venerate or seek succour and rest in, such places of pilgrimage, or who desire to observe Islamic rules of worship and prayer;

(c) to promote in general, the religious, cultural, educational and economic well being and Welfare of Muslims ;

(d) to provide relief and aid to the poor, the needy the sick and the aged of the Muslim faith visiting the Shrines and Thakiyas, and any other relief such as the provisions of social services, Educational and Training facilities of all manner and description to the Muslim Community in general ;

(e) to establish and maintain a Muslim religious school for the teaching of Quran and the Arabic Language; and

(f) do such other acts and things as are conducive or incidental to the attainment of all or any of the above objects.

General powers of the Corporation.

4. written law, the Corporation shall have the power to do, perform and execute all such acts, matters and things whatsoever, as are necessary or desirable for the promotion and furtherance of the objects of the Corporation or any one of them , including the power to open, operate or close bank accounts, to borrow or raise moneys, with or without security, to receive or collect grants and donations, to invest and expend its funds, and to engage employ and dismiss officers and servants required for the carrying out of the objects of Corporation.

Rules of the Corporation.

6. (1) It shall be lawful for the Corporation, from time to time, at any general meeting and by a majority of the members who are the descendants of the Seyed Zain Sakhaff Moulana family present and voting, to make rules not inconsistent with the provisions of this act and any other written law, for the admission , withdrawal and expulsion of members of the Corporation, for the election of the office-bearers and the Board of Trustees and their powers conduct and duties, for the procedure to be followed at the meetings of the Corporation and the Board of Trustees and generally, for the management of the affairs of the Corporation and the accomplishment of its objects. Such rules when made may, at a like meeting and in like manner be altered, added to, amended or rescinded.

(2) The members of the Corporation shall be subject to the rules of the Corporation.

Corporation may hold property movable or immovable.

7. The Corporation shall be able and capable in law, to acquire and hold any property, movable or immovable, which may become vested in it by virture of any purchase, grant, gift, testamentary disposition or otherwise and all such property shall be held by the Corporation for the purposes of this Act and subject to the rules in force for the time being of the Corporation, with full power to sell, mortgage, lease, exchange or otherwise dispose of the same.

Debts due by and payable to the Development Society.

All debts and liabilities of the Development Society existing on the day preceding the date of commencement of this Act, shall be paid by the Corporation hereby constituted and all debts due to and subscriptions and contributions payable to the Development society on that day shall be paid to the Corporation for the purposes of this Act.

Seal of the Corporation.

9. The seal of the Corporation shall not be affixed to any instrument whatsoever except in the presence at least of two members of the Board of Trustees who shall sign their names to the instrument in token of their presence, and such signing shall be independent of the signing of any person as a witness.

Saving of the rights of the Republic and others.

10. Nothing in this Act contained shall prejudice or affect the rights of the Republic, or of any body politic or corporate or of any other persons except such as are mentioned in this Act, and those claiming by, from, or under , them.

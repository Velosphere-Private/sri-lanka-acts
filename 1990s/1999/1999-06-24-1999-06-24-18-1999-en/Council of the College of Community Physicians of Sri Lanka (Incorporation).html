AN ACT TO INCORPORATE THE COUNCIL OF THE COLLEGE OF COMMUNITY PHYSICIANS OF SRI LANKA

Preamble.

WHEREAS a College called and Known as “ The College of Community Physicians of Sri Lanka” has heretofore been established in Sri Lanka;

AND WHEREAS the affairs and objects of the said College have heretofore been successfully managed and carried out by the council of the College.

AND WHEREAS the council of the college has applied to be incorporated and will be for the public advantage to grant the application.

BE it therefore enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:-

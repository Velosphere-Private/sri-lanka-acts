AN ACT TO AMEND THE FINANCE ACT, NO. 11 OF 2004

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—

Short title.

1. This Act may be cited as the Finance (Amendment) Act, No. 37 of 2007.

2. Part II (Cellular Mobile Telephone Subscribers’ Levy) Amendment of section 14 of Act, No. 11 of 2004.

of the Finance Act, No. 11 of 2004 is hereby amended in section 14 thereof by the substitution for the words “at the rate of two and half per centum” of the words “at the rate of ten per centum”.

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency.

3. In the event of an inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

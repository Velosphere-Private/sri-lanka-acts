AN ACT TO AMEND THE FINANCE LEASING ACT, NO. 56 OF 2000

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—

1. This Act may be cited as the Finance Leasing Short title. (Amendment) Act, No. 33 of 2007.

2. The following new section is hereby inserted Insertion of new immediately after section 34 of the Finance Leasing Act, No. section 34A in Act 56 of 2000 and shall have effect as section 34 of that Act :— No. 56 of 2000.

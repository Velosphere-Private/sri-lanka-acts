L.D.—O. 4/2008.

AN ACT TO AMEND THE FINANCE ACT, NO. 11 OF 2004

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—

Short title.

1. This Act may be cited as the Finance (Amendment) Act, No. 7 of 2008.

2. The Finance Act, No. 11 of 2004 is hereby amended in Amendment of Part II of Act, No. 11 of 2004.

Part II of that Act, (Imposition of Cellular Mobile Telephone Subscribers’ Levy) by the substitution for the words “mobile telephone” wherever such words occur in that Part, of the words “mobile or cordless land telephone”.

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and the Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—

Short title.

1. This act may be cited as the Betting and Gaming Levy (Amendment) Act, No. 9 of 2005.

Amendment of section 2 of the Betting and Gaming Levy Act, No. 40 of 1988.

2. Section 2 of the Betting and Gaming Levy Act, No. 40 of 1988 (hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) is hereby amended in subsection (1) thereof, by the substitution for the words “at the respective rates set out in the Schedule to this Act: ,” of the words “equivalent to the respective amounts set out in the Schedule to this Act:”

Sri Lanka Tea Board (Amendment) Act, No. 44 of 2006

[Certified on 06th November, 2006]

L.D.—O. 50/2004.

AN ACT TO AMEND THE SRI LANKA TEA BOARD LAW,

NO. 14 OF 1975

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—

1. This Act may be cited as the Sri Lanka Tea Board Short title. (Amendment) Act, No. 44 of 2006

No. 14 of 1975.

2. Section 6 of the Sri Lanka Tea Board Law, No. 14 of Amendment of 1975, is hereby amended in paragraph (b) of subsection (1) section 6 of Law, of section 6 by the repeal of sub-paragraph (iii) of that paragraph and the substitution therefor of the following subparagraphs :—

“(iii) a representative of the Ministry of the Minster in charge of the subject of Plantation Industries, nominated by that Minister ;”,

(iiia) one person nominated by the Minister in charge of the subject of Plantation Industries.”.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency.

and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

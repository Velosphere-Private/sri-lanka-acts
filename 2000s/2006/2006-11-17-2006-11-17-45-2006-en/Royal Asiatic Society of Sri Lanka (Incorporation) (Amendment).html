AN

ACT TO AMEND THE ROYAL ASIATIC SOCIETY OF SRI LANKA (INCORPORATION) ACT, NO. 6 OF 1992

Preamble.

WHEREAS it has become necessary, inter alia, to provide for the Corporation to be also able to engage in its activities more effectively and democratically and to provide for the Corporation to change the clauses of the Constitution to enable the Corporation and the Council to carry out its activities much more efficiently, smoothly and in keeping with the requirements needed to suit changing circumstances and the needs of changing times :

AND WHEREAS the Corporation has been required to effect the necessary amendments to the Royal Asiatic Society of Sri Lanka (Incorporation) Act to provide for the above requirements and it is expedient to effect such amendments.

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :-

Short title.

1. This Act may be cited as the Royal Asiatic Society of Sri Lanka (Incorporation) (Amendment) Act, No. 45 of 2006.

Amendment of section 4 (1) of the Royal Asiatic Society of Sri Lanka (Incorporation) Act.

2. Section 4(1) of the Royal Asiatic Society of Sri Lanka (Incorporation) Act, is hereby amended by the repeal of subsection (1) thereof and that substitution therefor of the following :—

“(1) The affairs of the Corporation shall subject to the rules and regulations in force for the time being of the Corporation be administered by a Council consisting of the office-bearers and such other members elected by the Corporation.”

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

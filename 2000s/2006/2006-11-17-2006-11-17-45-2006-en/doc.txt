AN ACT TO AMEND THE ROYAL ASIATIC SOCIETY OF SRI LANKA(INCORPORATION) ACT, NO. 6 OF 1992

WHEREAS it has become necessary, inter alia, to providefor the Corporation to be also able to engage in its activitiesmore effectively and democratically and to provide for theCorporation to change the clauses of the Constitution toenable the Corporation and the Council to carry out itsactivities much more efficiently, smoothly and in keepingwith the requirements needed to suit changing circumstancesand the needs of changing times :

AND WHEREAS the Corporation has been required to effectthe necessary amendments to the Royal Asiatic Society of SriLanka (Incorporation) Act to provide for the aboverequirements and it is expedient to effect such amendments.

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :-

1.This Act may be cited as the Royal Asiatic Society ofSri Lanka (Incorporation) (Amendment) Act, No. 45 of 2006.

2.Section 4(1) of the Royal Asiatic Society of Sri Lanka(Incorporation) Act, is hereby amended by the repeal ofsubsection (1) thereof and that substitution therefor of thefollowing :—

“(1) The affairs of the Corporation shall subject tothe rules and regulations in force for the time being ofthe Corporation be administered by a Councilconsisting of the office-bearers and such other memberselected by the Corporation.”

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

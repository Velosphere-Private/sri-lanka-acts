BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :-

Short title

1. This Act may be cited as the Rubber Control (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2025.

Chapter 436

2. (1) The Rubber Control Act (Chapter 436) (hereinafter General referred to as the “principal enactment”) is hereby amended Amendment to by the substitution for the words, “Controller” and “Rubber Controller” wherever such words appear in the principal enactment of the words, “Director-General”.

(2) Every reference to the “Controller” and “Rubber Controller” in any other written law or in any notice, notification, contract, communication, form, other instrument or document shall from and after the date of commencement of this Act be read and construed as a reference made to the “Director-General of the Rubber Development Department”.

3. Section 26 of the principal enactment is hereby Repeal of section 26 of the principal enactment

repealed.

Repeal of sections 36, 37, 38, 39, 39a and 50 of the principal enactment

5. Sections 36, 37, 38, 39, 39A and 50 of the principal enactment are hereby repealed.

Amendment of section 52 of the principal enactment

6. Section 52 of the principal enactment is hereby amended by the repeal of the definition of “Controller”, and the substitution therefor, of the following new definition :-

““Director-General” means the Director-General of the Rubber Development Department, appointed or deemed to have been appointed under this Act;”.

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency

7. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

Budgetary Relief Allowance of Workers (Amendment) Act, No. 9 of 2025 [Certified on 23rd of July, 2025] L.D.-O 20/2025

An Act to amend the Budgetary Relief Allowance of Workers Act, No. 36 of 2005

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows: -

1. (1) This Act may be cited as the Budgetary Relief Short title and date of operation

Allowance of Workers (Amendment) Act, No. 9 of 2025.

(2) The provisions of this Act shall be deemed to have come into operation on April 1, 2025.

2. Section 3 of the Budgetary Relief Allowance of Workers Amendment Act, No. 36 of 2005 is hereby amended in subsection (1) of section 3 of thereof, by the substitution for the words and figures "With Act, No. 36 of effect from August 1, 2005", of the words and figures "For the period commencing on August 1, 2005 and ending on March 31, 2025, ".

2005

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency

and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

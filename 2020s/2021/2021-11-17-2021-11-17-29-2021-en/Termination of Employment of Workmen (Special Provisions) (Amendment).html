Termination of Employment of Workmen (Special Provisions) (Amendment) Act, No. 29 of 2021

[Certified on 17th of November, 2021]

L.D.—O. 31/2021

AN ACT TO AMEND THE TERMINATION OF EMPLOYMENT OF WORKMEN (SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, NO. 45 OF 1971

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—

Short title

1. This Act may be cited as the Termination of Employment of Workmen (Special Provisions) (Amendment) Act, No. 29 of 2021.

Amendment of Section 3 of Act, No. 45 of 1971

2. Section 3 of the Termination of Employment of Workmen (Special Provisions) Act, No. 45 of 1971 is hereby amended in subsection (1) thereof, by the repeal of paragraph (c) and the substitution therefore of the following:-

“(c)

to the termination of employment of any workman where such termination was effected upon such workman attains the minimum retirement age as specified in the Minimum Retirement Age of Workers Act, No. 28 of 2021;”.

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

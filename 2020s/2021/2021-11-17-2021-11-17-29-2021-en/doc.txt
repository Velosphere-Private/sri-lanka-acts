Short title

[Certified on 17th of November, 2021]

L.D.—O. 31/2021

AN ACT TO AMEND THE TERMINATION OF EMPLOYMENT OF WORKMEN(SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, NO. 45 OF 1971

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:—

1.This Act may be cited as the Termination ofEmployment of Workmen (Special Provisions)(Amendment) Act, No. 29 of 2021.

2.Section 3 of the Termination of Employment ofWorkmen (Special Provisions) Act, No. 45 of 1971 is herebyamended in subsection (1) thereof, by the repeal of paragraph(c) and the substitution therefore of the following:-

“(c)to the termination of employment of anyworkman where such termination was effectedupon such workman attains the minimumretirement age as specified in the MinimumRetirement Age of Workers Act, No. 28 of2021;”.

3.In the event of any inconsistency between theSinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shallprevail.

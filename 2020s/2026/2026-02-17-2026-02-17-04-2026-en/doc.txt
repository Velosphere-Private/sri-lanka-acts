An Act to amend the Licensing of Shipping Agents, Freight

Forwarders, Non-Vessel Operating Common Carriers

and Container Operators Act, No. 10 of 1972; and to

provide for matters connected therewith or incidental

thereto.

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist

Republic of Sri Lanka as follows: -

1. This Act may be cited as the Licensing of Shipping

Agents, Freight Forwarders, Non-Vessel Operating

Common Carriers and Container Operators (Amendment)

Act, No. 4 of 2026.

2. The long title to the Licensing of Shipping Agents,

Freight Forwarders, Non-Vessel Operating Common

Carriers and Container Operators Act, No. 10 of 1972,

(hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) is

hereby amended by the substitution for the words “Freight

Forwarders, Non-Vessel Operating Common Carriers, and

Container Operators”, of the words “Freight Forwarders

and Non-Vessel Operating Common Carriers”.

Licensing of Shipping Agents, Freight Forwarders, Non-Vessel Operating Common Carriers and Container Operators (Amendment) Act, No. 4 of 2026

3. (1) In the principal enactment and in any other written law, there shall be substituted –

(a) for the words, “Licensing of Shipping Agents, Freight Forwarders, Non - Vessel Operating Common Carriers and Container Operators Act”, of the words “Licensing of Shipping Agents, Freight Forwarders and Non-Vessel Operating Common Carriers Act”; and

(b) for the words, “Shipping Agents, Freight Forwarders, Non - Vessel Operating Common Carriers, and Container Operators”, of the words “Shipping Agents, Freight Forwarders and Non-Vessel Operating Common Carriers”.

(2) Every reference to the “Licensing of Shipping Agents, Freight Forwarders, Non - Vessel Operating Common Carriers and Container Operators Act” in any notice, notification, contract, communication or other document shall be read and construed as a reference to the “Licensing of Shipping Agents, Freight Forwarders and Non-Vessel Operating Common Carriers Act”.

4. Section 15 of the principal enactment is hereby amended by the deletion of the definition of expression “container operators”.

5. With effect from the date of commencement of this Act, notwithstanding any provision in any other law-

(a) any application made for the issue of licence in respect of container operator

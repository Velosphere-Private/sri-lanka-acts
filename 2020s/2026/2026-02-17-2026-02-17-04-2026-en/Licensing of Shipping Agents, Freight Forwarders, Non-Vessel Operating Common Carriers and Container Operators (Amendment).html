(2) Every reference to the “Licensing of Shipping Agents, Freight Forwarders, Non - Vessel Operating Common Carriers and Container Operators Act” in any notice, notification, contract, communication or other document shall be read and construed as a reference to the “Licensing of Shipping Agents, Freight Forwarders and Non-Vessel Operating Common Carriers Act”.

Amendment of section 15 of the principal enactment

4. Section 15 of the principal enactment is hereby amended by the deletion of the definition of expression “container operators”.

Savings and transitional provisions

5. With effect from the date of commencement of this Act, notwithstanding any provision in any other law-

(a) any application made for the issue of licence in respect of container operator under subsection (1A) of section 3 of the principal enactment and pending on the day immediately preceding the date of commencement of this Act shall be deemed to be made under the provisions of the Licensing of Container Depot Operators Act, No. 3 of 2026; and (b) unless specifically suspended or cancelled in the manner provided for in the principal enactment, all licences issued to container operator, and subsisting or having effect on the day immediately preceding the date of commencement of this Act shall be deemed with effect from the date of commencement of this Act to be licences issued under the provisions of the Licensing of Container Depot Operators Act, No. 3 of 2026.

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency

6. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

An Act to amend the Universities Act, No. 16 of 1978

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows: -

1. This Act may be cited as the Universities (Amendment) Act, No. 2 of 2026.

2. Section 49 of the Universities Act, No.16 of 1978 (hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) is hereby amended as follows: -

(1) in subsection (1) of that section –

(a) by the re-lettering of that subsection as paragraph (a) of that subsection;

(b) by the substitution for all the words from “The Dean shall be elected” to the end of the re-lettered paragraph (a), of the following: -

“The Dean shall be elected by the Faculty Board from among the Senior Professors, Professors, Associate Professors and Senior Lecturers (Grade I), of the Faculty.”; and

(2) by the insertion immediately after the re-lettered paragraph (a) of subsection (1), of the following paragraph: -

“(b) Where any Senior Professor, Professor, Associate Professor or Senior Lecturer (Grade I) is elected as the Dean of the Faculty under paragraph (a), and is, at the time of election, holding the post of the Head of a Department of Study of that Faculty, such Senior Professor, Professor, Associate Professor or Senior Lecturer (Grade I) shall cease to hold the post of the Head of that Department of Study upon being elected as the Dean of the Faculty.”;

(3) in subsection (2) of that section, by the substitution for the words “unless removed from office,”, of the words “unless removed from office by the Council in accordance with the due procedure, as may be specified by the Commission,”;

(4) in subsection (3) of that section, by the substitution for the words “another Head of a Department of that Faculty”, of the words “another Senior Professor, Professor, Associate Professor or Senior Lecturer (Grade I), of that Faculty”; and

(5) by the addition immediately after subsection (3) of that section, of the following new subsection: -

“(4) No person shall be elected as the Dean of a Faculty for more than two terms.”.

3. Section 51 of the principal enactment is hereby amended as follows: -Amendment of section 51 of the principal enactment

(1) by the repeal of subsection (1) of that section and the substitution therefor, of the following subsection: -

“(1) (a) The Head of a Department of Study shall be a Senior Professor appointed by the Council upon the recommendation of the Vice-Chancellor.

(b) In the absence of a Senior Professor a Professor, an Associate Professor, a Senior Lecturer (Grade I) or a Senior Lecturer (Grade II) shall be appointed by the Council in that order, as the Head of a Department of Study, on the recommendation of the Vice-Chancellor.

(c) Prior to make any such appointment referred to in paragraph (a) or (b), the consent of the selected individual with respect to such appointment, shall be obtained.”;

(2) by the insertion immediately after subsection (1), of the following new subsection: -

“(1A) (a) For the purpose of subsection (1), any Senior Professor, Professor, Associate Professor, or any Senior Lecturer (Grade I) or any Senior Lecturer (Grade II), who has been confirmed in the relevant post, shall be eligible to be appointed as the Head of a Department of Study.

(b) In the absence of a Senior Professor Professor, Associate Professor, Senior Lecturer

Provided that, where all eligible persons in a Department of Study have completed their terms as the Head of that Department of Study in accordance with the preceding provisions and no other eligible persons are available for such appointment, a person who has previously held the post of the Head of that Department of Study may, with the consent of such person, be re-appointed to the post of the Head of that Department of Study. Such re-appointment shall be made in accordance with the procedure set out in subsection (1A):

Provided further, no person shall hold the post of the Head of a Department of Study for more than two consecutive terms.”.

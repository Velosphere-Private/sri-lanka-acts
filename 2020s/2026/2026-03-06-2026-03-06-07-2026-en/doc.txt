1Poisons, Opium and Dangerous Drugs (Amendment) Act, No. 7 of 2026

An Act To Amend The Poisons, Opium And Dangerous Drugs Ordinance (Chapter 218)

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows: -

1. This Act may be cited as the Poisons, Opium and Dangerous Drugs (Amendment) Act, No. 7 of 2026.

2. The following new section is hereby inserted immediately after section 54A of the Poisons, Opium and Dangerous Drugs Ordinance (Chapter 218) (hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) and shall have effect as section 54AA of that Ordinance:-

54AA. (1) Any person who, whether he is a citizen of Sri Lanka or not, on the high seas, while on board a ship registered in Sri Lanka or a stateless ship, manufactures any of the following dangerous drugs, namely heroin, cocaine, morphine or opium, shall be guilty of an offence and shall, on conviction by the High Court, be liable to a sentence of death or life imprisonment.

(2) Any person who, whether he is a citzen of Sri Lanka or not,-

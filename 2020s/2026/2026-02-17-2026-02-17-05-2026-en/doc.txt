PARLIAMENTARY PENSIONS (REPEAL)

ACT, No. 5 OF 2026

[Certified on 17th of February, 2026]

Printed on the Order of Government

Published as a Supplement to Part II of the Gazette of the Democratic

Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka of February 20, 2026

Parliamentary Pensions (Repeal)

Act, No. 5 of 2026

3

[Certified on 17th of February, 2026]

L.D.-O 31/2025

An Act to repeal the Parliamentary Pensions

Law, No. 1 of 1977

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows: -

1. This Act may be cited as the Parliamentary Pensions (Repeal) Act, No. 5 of 2026.

2. The Parliamentary Pensions Law, No. 1 of 1977 is hereby repealed.

3. For the avoidance of doubt, it is hereby declared that any person who is entitled to receive a pension in terms of the provisions of the Parliamentary Pensions Law, No. 1 of 1977, section 9 of Act, No. 1 of 1982 or section 9 of Act, No. 47 of 1990, and is in receipt of a pension under the aforesaid provisions preceding the date of commencement of this Act, shall, on and after the date of commencement of this Act, cease to receive such pension.

4. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

Short title

Repeal of the Law, No. 1 of 1977

Avoidance of doubt

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency

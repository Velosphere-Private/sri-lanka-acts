L.D.-O 38/2024

An Act to Amend the JudicAture Act, No. 2 of 1978

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows: -

1. This Act may be cited as the Judicature (Amendment) Act, Short title No. 8 of 2026.

Act, No. 2 of 1978

2. Section 9 of the Judicature Act, No. 2 of 1978 is hereby Amendment amended, by the repeal of paragraph (d) of subsection (1) of that of section 9 of section, and substitution therefor, of the following paragraph:-

“(d) any offence committed by any person on the high seas where-

(i) such offence is piracy by the law of nations; or

(ii) such offence is an offence under the Poisons, Opium and Dangerous Drugs Ordinance (Chapter 218), Conventions Against Illicit Traffic in Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, No. 1 of 2008 or any such written law;”.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency

Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

L.D.—O. 59/2019

AN ACT TO AMEND THE NATION BUILDING TAX

ACT, NO. 9 OF 2009

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows: -

Short title.

1. This Act may be cited as the Nation Building Tax (Amendment) Act, No. 3 of 2020.

Amendment of section 3 of Act, No. 9 of 2009.

2. Section 3 of the Nation Building Tax Act, No. 9 of 2009 as last amended by Act, No. 20 of 2019, is hereby further amended in subsection (1) by the substitution for the words, “be charged from every person”, of the words and figures “be charged prior to December 1, 2019, from every person”.

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
